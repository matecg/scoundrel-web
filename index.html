Scoundrel

Rules

👉 Each suit represent a special dungeon entity. To either aid or harm you.

👉 Your goal is to explore all the dungeon's rooms and survive them all.

👉 You will lose if your health reaches zero.

  • Rooms:
    • Are represented by the 4 topmost cards from the deck.
    • You must choose and interact with 3 of them in order to complete a room.
    • The left over card will be carried into the next room.
    • You may also Skip a room entirely, sending those cards to the bottom of the deck.
    • But, you may not skip two rooms in a roll.
  • ♥️ Hearts represent Potions:
    • Range from rank 2 to 10.
    • Can be used only once per room.
    • Additional potions consumed on the same room will simply be discarded.
  • ♦️ Diamonds represent Weapons:
    • Range from rank 2 to 10.
    • Used in combat against creatures to lower the damage received.
    • Equipping a new weapon automatically discards the previous.
    • After killing a monster, the creatures power is added to durability, as a new maximum.
    • It can only kill a monster with a value lower than the last monster it killed (or its initial value).
    • You may always fight barehanded, taking all the damage from the creature to defeat it.
  • ♣️|♠️ Clubs & Spades represent Creatures:
    • Range from rank 2 to Ace.
    • A monster power is represented by the card value.
    • "J" = 11, "Q" = 12, "K" = 13, and "A" = 14.

Your final score starts at zero and gets deduced by the value of each creature you haven't defeated.

Alternatively, it will be increased by the remaining health points.

Also, if your last card, left in the dungeon, is a potion, its value will be added to your final score.

That way the maximum score achievable is: 30 (20 points from health + 10 points from a non-used potion)

Be wary, be smart. Best of luck!
